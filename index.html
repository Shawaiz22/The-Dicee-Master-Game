GamePlay Guide

Consider following points

  • You will compete with your opponent, either one of your friend (in 2P) mode, or with computer.
  • You will be given a chance to roll your dice and on that basis your score will increase as according to the number obtained on the dice
  • Gradually, your score will keep on increasing in every pass, you and your opponent will be given chance to alternately throw the dice
  • But, the special thing here is that, you will be provide two set of values (obstacles) and (assets), each set will have 5 different values ranging between 1 and 99. If player steps(reaches to the position) on any point which is inside the obstacle set, his points will be deducted üòê.
    The amount of deduction will be equal to the position of that value inside that set. If on position 1 then (score -1) and so on....
    Ex- Assets=[12, 23, 31, 43, 89]
    Lets consider a set of asset values,
    Now, if any user steps on position=12, his score(position), will increment by 1 and if on 43, it increment by 4, as according to the value positions(indexes)
  • Similarly, if a user steps on any of the asset values, his score will increment ü§©! incremention rules will be as similar to the decremention rule!
  • In this way, in the end the first user which is able to reach till position 100, wins the game! ü§©
